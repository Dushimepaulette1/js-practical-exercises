Crocheting

Crochet is a craft technique that involves using a hooked needle to create fabric by interlocking loops of yarn or thread. It is similar to knitting, but instead of using two needles, crochet uses a single hook.

Why crochet?

Some of these restorative benefits of crocheting include combating anxiety, relieving stress, reducing insomnia, building self-esteem, depression relief, improving fine motor skills, decreasing the risk of Alzheimer's, and so much more. Creating art with yarn by crocheting and knitting can transform you.
